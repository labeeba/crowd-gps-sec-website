Crowd-GPS-Sec

Crowd-GPS-Sec is a solution proposed to detect and localize GPS spoofing attacks on moving airborne targets such as UAVs or commercial airliners. The GPS Spoofing detection module consists of two tests:

  1. Test 1 - Cross Checks with MLAT Condition: MLAT requires atleast 4 sensors on the ground to receive ADS-B messages.
  2. Test 2 - Multiple Aircraft Comparison Condition: Aircraft Comparison requires atleast 2 aircrafts to receive the spoofed signal.
